Create a NEW ENTRY / LANDING PAGE for the
"AI & Workforce Intelligence Platform".

This page is NOT a dashboard.
It is an ORIENTATION & DISCOVERY PAGE.

The goal is to:
- immediately reframe fear around AI and work
- clearly explain what this platform helps users understand
- invite users into exploration paths
- feel modern, calm, and intellectually serious

================================
CORE PRODUCT PRINCIPLE
================================
Users should feel:
"Okay, this is interesting. I want to explore."

NOT:
"I need to understand charts."

================================
PAGE STRUCTURE
================================

--------------------------------
SECTION 1 — HERO (NO CHARTS)
--------------------------------

Centered content. Clean. Calm.

MAIN HEADLINE (large, bold):
"AI is not replacing most jobs.
It is reshaping tasks, skills, and expectations."

SUBHEADLINE (1–2 lines):
"This platform helps you understand how AI is changing work —
based on tasks, skills, and real labor-market evidence."

SMALL DISCLAIMER (subtle):
"Explains structural patterns. Does not predict individual outcomes."

Optional subtle background visual (abstract, not data-heavy).

--------------------------------
SECTION 2 — WHAT THIS PLATFORM DOES (VERY SHORT)
--------------------------------

Three short bullets or icons:

- Task-based analysis (not job loss headlines)
- Research-backed insights (OECD, IAB, labor economics)
- Calm explanations, not predictions or advice

Keep this tight. No jargon.

--------------------------------
SECTION 3 — CHOOSE HOW YOU WANT TO EXPLORE (KEY SECTION)
--------------------------------

Display THREE LARGE, CLICKABLE CARDS
(equal size, visually strong, plenty of space).

--------------------------------
CARD 1 — EXPLORE BY ROLE
--------------------------------

Title:
"Explore roles like mine"

Description:
"See how AI typically affects tasks, skills, and demand
across roles and sectors."

What users will see (small caption):
"Task transformation • Skill change • Demand context"

CTA Button:
"Explore roles →"

On click:
Navigate to the main role-based dashboard view.

--------------------------------
CARD 2 — UNDERSTAND MY TASKS (PRIMARY CTA)
--------------------------------

Visually emphasize this card slightly more than others.

Title:
"Understand what happens to my tasks"

Description:
"Select a role or confirm typical tasks.
See which parts of the work are supported by AI,
automated, or remain human."

Caption:
"Job-Futuromat-style • No CV • No predictions"

CTA Button:
"Describe my role →"

On click:
Navigate to the role → task → AI impact flow.

--------------------------------
CARD 3 — SEE THE BIG PICTURE
--------------------------------

Title:
"Understand the bigger picture"

Description:
"A research-based view of how AI is reshaping
skills, hiring, and labor demand overall."

Caption:
"Trends • Patterns • Context"

CTA Button:
"See the big picture →"

On click:
Navigate to a high-level narrative / explainer page
(no detailed dashboard yet).

--------------------------------
SECTION 4 — TRUST & POSITIONING (SUBTLE)
--------------------------------

Small text block or footer-style content:

- "Built on labor-market research, not hype"
- "Task- and skill-based, not job predictions"
- "Designed for workers, migrants, educators, and policymakers"

Optional links:
- Methodology
- Sources
- About
