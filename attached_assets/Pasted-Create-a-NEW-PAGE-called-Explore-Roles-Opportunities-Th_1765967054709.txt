Create a NEW PAGE called:

"Explore Roles & Opportunities"

This page helps users understand
HOW roles, skills, and industries are shifting under AI —
without giving career advice or predictions.

This is a STRUCTURAL EXPLORATION tool, not a recommendation engine.

================================
CORE USER QUESTION
================================
"What kinds of roles and skills are becoming more relevant,
and which ones face higher change pressure?"

================================
DESIGN PRINCIPLES
================================
- Calm, research-based framing
- No personal scoring
- No 'you should' language
- Show patterns, not prescriptions
- Emphasize opportunity AND adaptation pressure together

================================
PAGE STRUCTURE
================================

--------------------------------
SECTION 1 — ORIENTATION (NO DATA YET)
--------------------------------

Title:
"How AI is reshaping roles and opportunities"

Short explanation (2–3 lines):
"This view shows how roles and skill profiles are shifting
based on task transformation, skill demand, and labor-market signals.
It highlights patterns — not individual outcomes."

Small disclaimer:
"These are structural trends, not hiring predictions."

--------------------------------
SECTION 2 — FILTER & EXPLORE CONTEXT
--------------------------------

Provide FILTER CONTROLS at the top:

- Sector (dropdown)
  ["Health", "IT", "Logistics", "Administration", "Education", "Manufacturing"]

- Role Group (dropdown)
  ["Analytical", "Program & Ops", "Service", "Technical", "Support"]

- Region (dropdown)
  ["Germany", "Europe", "Global"]

Optional toggle:
"Compare with another role"

--------------------------------
SECTION 3 — ROLE LANDSCAPE OVERVIEW (KEY VISUAL)
--------------------------------

Display a GRID or LIST of ROLE CARDS
(based on selected filters).

Each ROLE CARD shows:

- Role name
- Sector
- THREE STRUCTURAL SIGNALS (icons or labels):

  1) Task impact:
     Mostly augmenting / Mixed / Higher automation exposure

  2) Skill change intensity:
     Low / Moderate / High

  3) Demand signal:
     Growing / Stable / Declining

Use calm labels, no scores.

--------------------------------
SECTION 4 — OPPORTUNITY & CHANGE PRESSURE VIEW
--------------------------------

When a role card is clicked, open a DETAIL PANEL.

Title:
"Structural context for this role"

Include TWO clearly separated blocks:

BLOCK A — Opportunity surfaces
- Examples:
  - "AI supports core tasks rather than replacing them"
  - "Skill clusters remain in demand across sectors"
  - "Role aligns with growing or stable labor demand"

BLOCK B — Change pressure
- Examples:
  - "Increased expectations for analytical or digital fluency"
  - "Routine elements increasingly supported by AI tools"
  - "Higher adaptability required over time"

Explicit sentence:
"Opportunity and change pressure often coexist."

--------------------------------
SECTION 5 — SKILL CLUSTERS (NOT SKILLS LIST)
--------------------------------

Show inferred SKILL CLUSTERS for the role
(based on task profiles).

Examples:
- Analytical reasoning
- Coordination & communication
- Documentation & synthesis
- Domain knowledge
- Judgment & decision-making

For each cluster, show:
- Typical AI interaction: Supportive / Complementary / Minimal

NO skill-level assessment.
NO learning recommendations.

--------------------------------
SECTION 6 — CONTEXTUAL COMPARISON (OPTIONAL)
--------------------------------

Allow users to compare:
- two roles
OR
- role vs role group average

Show differences in:
- task mix
- skill change intensity
- demand signal

Use simple side-by-side labels.

--------------------------------
SECTION 7 — WHAT THIS MEANS (INTERPRETATION)
--------------------------------

Mandatory interpretation block:

Title:
"How to read these signals"

Include:
- 3 bullets explaining how opportunity signals emerge
- 2 bullets explaining limits and uncertainty
- Explicit note:
  "This view supports orientation and exploration,
   not career or hiring decisions."

--------------------------------
DATA & LOGIC REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------

Use mock but realistic data from:

/data
- role_task_profiles.json
- indicators_by_role.csv
- demand_signals.csv

Logic should be:
- rule-based
- explainable
- reusable across pages

NO ML.
NO personalization.
